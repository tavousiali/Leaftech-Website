We provide
the missing data

We upgrade the performance of your single devices and complex automation systems in 3 simple steps.

3D Modelling
with static data

We model your building and its surroundings in three dimensions.
We get started with any data you provide us and add missing information layer by layer.

Capturing dynamic data
with virtual sensors

Taking in consideration your system and the data points you need to measure, we generate virtual sensors anywhere on this digital representation.

Our virtual sensors enable you to monitor the dynamic influence factors such as the effects of weather influence on the building envelope.
(Temperature, wind, solar radiation..)

Providing data
for optimised control

We merge the dynamic and static data threads with our machine learning algorithms to form a digital twin of the building. In the next step, taking advantage of machine learning algorithms the energy consumption of the building is predicted.

You can choose which services you specifically need to ensure optimal conditions.
